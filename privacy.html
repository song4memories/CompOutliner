Comp Outliner — Privacy Policy

English

Last updated: 2026-07-18

Comp Outliner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app has no analytics, no tracking, and no account system. Documents you create and edit stay on your device; Comp Outliner never uploads them. App settings, templates, and your recent-files list are stored only on your Mac. If you contact us for support, we use your message solely to respond.

Contact: song4memories@gmail.com

Comp Outliner — プライバシーポリシー

日本語

最終更新: 2026-07-18
